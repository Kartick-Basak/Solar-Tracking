üí° Project Overview

This system uses Light Dependent Resistors (LDRs) and an IOT Microcontroller (like ESP32/Arduino) to continuously adjust the position of a solar panel. The goal is to keep the panel perpendicular to the sun's rays for maximum energy generation.

The **Automatic Tracking Mode** uses a feedback loop to find the position of highest solar intensity. The **Manual Control Mode** allows remote calibration.
